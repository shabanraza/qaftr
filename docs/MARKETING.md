## Positioning

Qaftr helps Saudi freelancers and small businesses create simplified VAT invoices with ZATCA-compliant QR data, PDF export, and WhatsApp sharing.

Do not present Qaftr as an official ZATCA app. Use "ZATCA-compliant" or "for Saudi VAT invoices" instead of wording that implies government affiliation.

## Notes From Research

- Arabic terms have stronger Saudi search-volume signals than English ZATCA terms.
- `فاتورة ضريبية` should appear in the Arabic app name and landing hero.
- `الفاتورة الالكترونية` should appear in the Arabic subtitle and landing content.
- Keep `ZATCA` in subtitle, keywords, description, and review notes rather than making it the first word of the app name.
- Generic words such as `invoice`, `VAT`, `QR`, `reader`, and `scanner` are common in store listings, but the full app name and metadata must not look copycat or official.
